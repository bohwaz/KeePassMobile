Upcoming Version V0.9

Posted by Christoph on 10 April 2010 | 4 Comments

Tags: release, V0.9

I have been very busy at the beginning of the year. That is, why the KeePassMobile development stuck the last three months, sorry. I changed my job and work for Canoo now. Canoo has a very good reputation in the Java community and I am proud that they chose me. There are so many new things to learn for me.

However, in the meanwhile I have settled really well and I started to develop some new features for KeePassMobile V0.9 already. This release will not contain much new features, but will stabilize the V0.8 release, fix some problems and add minor missing things to fill the gaps of the feature list.

Thats for example:

  • Move password entries between groups
  • Automatically backup password entries, when changed
  • Integrate a password generator
  • ...???...

Do you have an other important feature on your wish list? Leave a note on the forum.
