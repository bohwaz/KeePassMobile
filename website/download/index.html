Download

There are two different ways to download KeePassMobile and install it on your mobile phone.

Computer Download

Download the latest release of KeePassMobile to your PC. The installation on your mobile phone depends on the software of the mobile manufacturer. If you have problems, take a look/leave a note in the "Installation Guide"-forums.

Mobile Download

Open the browser of your mobile phone, navigate to http://m.keepassmobile.com and follow the instructions on the screen of your mobile.

Attention: this downloads approximately 440kb from the internet directly to your mobile phone. Dependent on your mobile phone contract this is not free of costs.

KeePassMobile LIGHT for old/low-end mobiles

The standard package of KeePassMobile is very resource hungry and runs not well on old/low-end mobiles. If you own such a mobile than try the LIGHT version of KeePassMobile. Download the latest release of KeePassMobile LIGHT to your PC and install it like the standard package. Please leave a quick note in the forum, if the LIGHT version works for your mobile. See the FAQ on how KeePassMobile LIGHT differs from KeePassMobile.
