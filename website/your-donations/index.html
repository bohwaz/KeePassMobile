Your donations

Posted by Christoph on 28 May 2010 | 0 Comments

Tags: donations

Probably you know this already: KeePassMobile is developed by me in my rare free time and is free and open source. There is a donation page on this website, that states:

As you probably know, developing high-quality applications takes much time and resources. So, if you find yourself using KeePassMobile regularly and if you are happy with the quality and the progress of this project, you are kindly requested to donate. Your donation will support the development of KeePassMobile and make me feel to produce something valuable.

To prove you, that these are not just empty words: I am learning for the Sun Certified Java Programmer (SCJP) exam at the moment and from your donations I have bought me this study guide and a bunch of mock exams to get fit for the exam. Altogether, this is more or less the sum of you donations so far and I want to say thanks to all donators!
