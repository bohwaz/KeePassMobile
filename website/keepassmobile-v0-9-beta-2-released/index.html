KeePassMobile V0.9 BETA 2 released

Posted by Christoph on 7 August 2010 | 6 Comments

Tags: release, beta, V0.9

Here is the second BETA release of the upcoming KeePassMobile V0.9. This release should be quite stable by now. Anyhow, always keep a backup of your password database elsewhere.

ATTENTION: THOUGH I INVESTED MUCH TIME IN TESTING KEEPASSMOBILE, A BETA VERSION IS NEVER AS STABLE AS THE FINAL RELEASE. THERE IS A SMALL BUT INCREASED RISK COMPARED TO A FINAL VERSION THAT KEEPASSMOBILE BETA CORRUPTS YOUR DATABASE. THEREFORE, ALWAYS CREATE A BACKUP OF YOUR DATABASE BEFORE USING THE BETA VERSION OF KEEPASSMOBILE!

If you download, install and test this version, please don't forget to leave a small note in the forum like: "Works!", "Does not start!", "Crashes, when..." with the name of your mobile.

There are two ways to install KeePassMobile V0.9 BETA:

  • Download the archive and install KeePassMobile manually: KeePassMobile-0.9-beta2.zip
  • OTA: Open the browser of your mobile phone, navigate to http://m2.keepassmobile.com and follow the instructions on the screen. Cheers, Christoph
