Download statistics after four months

Posted by Christoph on 16 July 2009 | 3 Comments

Tags: downloads, statistics, poll, donations

I started KeePassMobile about 4 months ago. Here are the download statistics for this time:

  • V0.2 (was available for 6 weeks, until V0.5 was released): 1996 downloads
  • V0.5 (was available for 11 weeks, until V0.6 was released): 5702 downloads
  • V0.6 (is available for 3 weeks now): 1818 downloads
  • In summary: 9516 downloads

Here is are the donation statistics for the same time:

  • V0.2: 0 donations
  • V0.5: 1 donation
  • V0.6: 0 donations
  • In summary: 1 donation

To keep up development speed, this project costs me much time and money (domain, homepage, traffic, ...) and so I am seriously considering to charge the next big feature that will come with the next version (V0.8): save/encrypt KeePass databases. My plan is not to sell KeePassMobile. You will be able to use all basic functions (load, decrypt, search,...) without paying a cent, but to charge activating the advanced features.

KeePassMobile would remain free of course, but free as in freedom of speech and not free as in free beer any more.

I set up a poll to hear your voice on this topic (poll closed in the meanwhile)
